FAQ

Common Questions

How is this different from a todo list? +
Todo lists track what to do. Decision Engine tracks what you decided, why you decided it, and whether it worked. It's a decision journal that learns.
Can my team use this? +
The open source version is single-user. Team collaboration is coming in Pro (Q2 2026). For now, each teammate can run their own instance.
Where is my data stored? +
By default, everything stays in your browser (localStorage). You own your data completely. Optional cloud sync coming in Pro tier.
Can I integrate with Slack/Notion/my CRM? +
Yes! The open source version supports webhooks. Every decision can POST to any URL. Build your own integrations or use our templates.
