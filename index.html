The Decision Loop

Four steps to better decisions. Every time you complete the loop, the system learns what works for you.

1
⚡

Make the Decision

Swipe right to approve, left to reject. Add context in the notes field. Your decision is instantly captured and webhooked to your system.

2
⏳

Time Passes

You execute on the decision. Book the flight. Hire the person. Launch the project. The task waits in "pending" status.

3
🔄

Report Back

When you're done, report what actually happened. Did you book United instead of Southwest? Save money? Hit a snag? Tag the outcome.

4
📚

System Learns

Your feedback builds a knowledge base. Next time a similar decision comes up, you (or your AI) knows what actually worked.

Built for Speed & Learning

Every feature designed to make decision-making faster while capturing institutional knowledge.

👆

Tinder-Style Swiping

Left to reject, right to approve. Keyboard shortcuts (← → arrows) for desktop. Decisions in under 2 seconds.

📝

Context Capture

Add notes to every decision. "Use points if available" or "Book earliest flight." Captured for future reference.

🔔

Instant Webhooks

Every decision fires a webhook. Send to Telegram, Slack, your CRM, or a custom endpoint. Stay in the loop.

🏷️

Outcome Tagging

Tag completions: "Did it differently," "Saved money," "Better approach found." Build patterns over time.

⚠️

Stale Detection

Tasks auto-flag as "stale" after 3 days without feedback. Nothing falls through the cracks.

💾

Offline First

Works without internet. Data stores locally, syncs when connected. Never lose a decision.

🎨

Customizable Cards

Edit the JSON array to add your own decisions. Customize categories, priorities, styling. Make it yours.

🚀

One-Click Deploy

Deploy to Vercel in 30 seconds. No backend setup required. Optional Telegram notifications.
